Systems - Races

Description

The race system is not active yet. It is planned to be activated in September 2023. The idea of the race system is simple and effective. Players will play the game via human body. It will not change. But players will have a mixed blood DNA and some extra skills depending on the DNA type. To have these DNAs, players will have to obtain the bloods of the certain races in the game and consume them to apply the DNAs to have the special skills.

You can view the planned races below for obtaining their blood and apply the DNAs for special skills

Elf Gargoyle Human
